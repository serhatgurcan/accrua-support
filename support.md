### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device only. We don't have access to your financial information.

**Q: How do I get Premium features?**
A: Go to Settings > Premium to view subscription options. Premium unlocks unlimited income sources, installments, savings goals, and more.

**Q: How do I backup my data?**
A: Use the Export feature in More > Export Data to save your data as CSV or PDF.

**Q: Can I use the app on multiple iPhones?**
A: With iCloud sync enabled, your data syncs across all your iPhones.

**Q: How do I add an expense with voice?**
A: In the Expenses tab, tap the microphone icon and say something like "150 lira market" - the app will automatically parse it.

**Q: What do the widgets show?**
A: Widgets display your real-time earnings progress and budget status. Add them from your home screen.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage your subscription.
